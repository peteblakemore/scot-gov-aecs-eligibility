Apply for the Agri-Environment Climate Scheme (AECS)

Follow these steps to complete your AECS application.

  1. 1 Register with RPID
    • Register with RPID to get a Business Reference Number (BRN)

      Register with RPID Online

    • Consider if AECS suits your business needs

      Full Scheme Guidance

  2. 2 Understand options and requirements
    • Check available options:
      • Check scheme options for your holding
      • View management options and capital items
    • If needed, get an endorsement from NatureScot
  3. 3 Prepare your application
    • Complete a Farm Environment Assessment (FEA)

      FEA guidance

    • Gather supporting documents:
      • Habitat assessments
      • Photographs
      • Required consents
    • Contact NatureScot if:
      • Your land is within a designated site
      • You are managing deer
      • You need an option endorsement
  4. 4 Submit your application

    Complete and submit your application through Rural Payments and Services:

    • Log in to RPS
    • Select "Agri-Environment and Climate Scheme"
    • Fill in required information
    • Upload supporting documents
    • Review and submit

    Apply online

  5. 5 After submission

    If successful:

    • Receive your AECS contract
    • Sign and return contract acceptance
    • Wait for "permission to commence" letter

    If unsuccessful:

    You can request a review of the decision

  6. 6 Managing your contract
    • Submit annual claims
    • Keep your management diary up to date
    • Request variations if your circumstances change
